Indigenous Cow Breeds of India

Detailed Breed Information

Gir Cow

Native to Gujarat, the Gir cow is renowned for its exceptional milk production and heat resistance. These cows are known for their distinctive appearance with large, drooping ears and a prominent forehead.

  • 🥛 Average Milk Yield: 3500-4000 kg per lactation
  • 🌞 Highly adaptable to tropical climates
  • 🧬 Genetic purity maintained through careful breeding

Sahiwal Cow

Originating from Punjab, Sahiwal cows are prized for their high milk production and docile nature. They are considered one of the best dairy breeds in India, known for their excellent milk quality.

  • 🥛 Average Milk Yield: 2500-3000 kg per lactation
  • 🤲 Gentle temperament, easy to handle
  • 🌿 Excellent foragers, low maintenance

Red Sindhi Cow

From the Sindh region, Red Sindhi cows are celebrated for their adaptability and consistent milk production. They have a distinctive reddish-brown coat and are highly valued in dairy farming.

  • 🥛 Average Milk Yield: 2000-2500 kg per lactation
  • 🌍 Widely distributed across India
  • 💪 Strong immunity and disease resistance

Tharparkar Cow

Native to the Thar Desert region of Rajasthan, Tharparkar cows are known for their remarkable ability to survive in harsh, arid conditions while maintaining good milk production.

  • 🥛 Average Milk Yield: 2000-2500 kg per lactation
  • 🏜️ Exceptional desert adaptability
  • 💧 Low water and feed requirements

Kangeyam Cow

Originating from Tamil Nadu, Kangeyam cows are robust draft animals with excellent strength and endurance. They play a crucial role in traditional agricultural practices.

  • 💪 Exceptional draft animal capabilities
  • 🌾 Vital in traditional farming
  • 🧬 Unique genetic characteristics

Hallikar Cow

From Karnataka, Hallikar cows are known for their incredible strength and endurance. They are excellent draft animals and have been traditionally used in agriculture and transportation.

  • 🚜 Superior draft animal qualities
  • 🌄 Well-adapted to hilly terrains
  • 🔧 Critical in rural agricultural systems
